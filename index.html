mini0906 dashcam GPS coordinate converter

The mini0906 dashcam (and probably others) burn coordinates into the video that resemble the common "decimal degree" notation used by most services nowadays, but unfortunately aren't compatible with it and many kilometers off the actual position. This tool is a handy converter into decimal degrees.
