mini0906 dashcam GPS coordinate converter

The mini0906 dashcam (and probably others) burn coordinates into the video that look similar to the common "decimal degree" notation used by most services nowadays. Unfortunately the burnt-in coordinates aren't compatible with the rest of the world and many kilometers off the actual position. This tool tries to be a handy converter into decimal degrees.
